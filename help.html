Help

To play a media file:

  1. If you are not already on the home page, click the “Home” tab in the top left corner.
  2. Click the “Media” tab.
  3. Click on the title of a media file.
  4. Click the “Done” button.
  5. Click play.

To change the media library directory:

  1. If you are not already on the home page, click the “Home” tab in the top left corner.
  2. Click on the gear, located in the top right corner.
  3. Type in the name of the directory that your media is located in.
  4. Click the “Done” button.

How to download a media file:

  1. If you are not already on the home page, click the “Home” tab in the top left corner.
  2. Click the "Media" tab.
  3. Click on the download icon that is next to the title of the media file you want to download.
